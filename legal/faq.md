# Frequently Asked Questions

To add a question, add a `## ` heading and an answer below it.

## Do I need to trim my GPS file to just the race?

No. Upload your whole session — warmup and cooldown included. The system
automatically finds the fastest valid segment between the course's start and
finish lines and uses that as your time, so there's no need to edit your file
first.

## What file formats can I upload?

GPX, FIT, and CSV files are all supported, and you can import an activity
directly from Strava once you've connected your account. Export your activity
from Garmin Connect, Strava, Apple Fitness, or any GPS device.

## Why wasn't my time recorded?

Your track didn't cross the course's start and finish lines (or, for gate
courses, every gate in the right order and direction) as configured. The
upload page shows a diagnostic map of your recorded track against the course
lines so you can see what happened — usually the GPS wasn't recording through
one of the lines, or the activity went a different way than the course
expects.

## Are heart rate and cadence stored?

No. Heart rate and cadence are intentionally discarded when your file is
parsed, even if the source file contains them. Only position and time are kept.

## Why are different boat classes on the same leaderboard?

Every entry records its boat class, and the leaderboard can be filtered to a
single class. Comparing across classes — say a K1 to an 8+ — isn't meaningful,
so filter to your own class to see where you stand.

## Who can see my time?

It depends on the course's visibility. Public courses and their trials are
visible to anyone; private courses are visible only to their owner. When you
make a trial public the Terms of Service note that your performance times may
become visible to others.
